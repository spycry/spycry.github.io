CLAIM YOUR SECURITY BACK AGAIN

Spycry is a mobile app which keeps your private data really private. With a single click, you can add different types of entries like notes, credit cards, logins and passwords, images and etc. and Spycry will encrypt and persist them securely for you. The data is encrypted with 256 bit AES encryption algorithm and can be decrypted only with using Master Password. Even is your phone gets hacked - the data will still not be compromised.

Spycry can upload your encrypted data to Google Drive and will keep it in sync across multiple devices. Your data is never stored unencrypted and is not shared with any thirdparties.

Features:

  • 256bit AES Encryption
  • Sync with Google Drive
  • Different types of entries: notes, credit cards, logins
  • Image attachments
  • Quick search
  • Fingerprint authentication
  • Modern UI designed with productivity in mind
