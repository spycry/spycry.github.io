What is Spycry

Spycry is a wallet and password manager that keeps your data secured. Save credit cards, logins, passwords, ID numbers, images and many more with a single tap. Your data is always encrypted and 100% protected. The only way to access your data - is to know the Master Password.

What is the Master Password

The Master Password is chosen by you and never leaves your device. Well, it’s even never stored on the device. Spycry has hints and tips how to make your Master Password very strong and secure.

Secure backup

Backup your encrypted data to Google Drive and sync it across any number of devices. Your data is never stored unencrypted. The data is visible only to you and Spycry doesn’t save any copies.

P.S.

Spycry will never share user data with any thirdparties.
